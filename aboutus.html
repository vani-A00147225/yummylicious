All About Yummylicious

Yummylicious is a team of people working together to make the world drool on Indian cuisine. We are dedicated to bringing authentic Indian food recipes in your kitchen.

Our Journey

Founded in the year-2012. The Yummylicious team is a group of chefs working in a five-star hotel in Hyderabad, India. The team of 3 chefs came along and founded this amazing platform where we can share our authentic Indian recipes. Yummylicious started its own Youtube channel in 2018 and has 5M followers on it. You can subscribe to Yummylicious on youtube as well.
